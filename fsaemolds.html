FSAE Composites — UCI Anteater Electric Racing

Project Overview

Took charge of leveling up Anteater Electric Racing's composites manufacturing — researching and documenting the resin infusion process end to end and running numerous infusion tests to refine the workflow.

Built a versatile tooling design plan in Fusion 360 for producing the carbon fiber aerobody parts that make up the car's bodywork, laying groundwork the team can reuse across future seasons.
