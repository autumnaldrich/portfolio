Project 01 — FSAE Aerodynamics

Drag Reduction System (DRS)

Leading research, design, and prototyping of a Drag Reduction System for the rear wing of UCI's Formula SAE Electric Racing Team — rotating the wing elements out of high-downforce mode on straightaways to cut drag.

The prototype targets the rear wing first, but lays the groundwork for active aero across the whole car, balancing downforce and drag through corners and straights.

Drag Reduction80–90%
Linkage Material1/4" 6061 Aluminum
FEA Safety Factor1.77 – 2.30

CAD linkage geometry rotates the mid airfoil from 27.5° down to 2.5°, and the top foil from 55° to 2.5°. STAR-CCM+ simulations of high-drag vs. low-drag modes informed the target angles, and SolidWorks FEA (worst-case ~400N load) confirmed the linkages hold under max actuation force.
